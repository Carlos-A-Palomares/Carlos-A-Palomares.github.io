Prologue: Under the celestial canopy, amidst the whispered legends of the old university grounds, lies the ancient and enigmatic sculpture known as the Stone Enclosure. Created by the visionary artist Nancy Holt, this structure is not just art but a gateway to the cosmos, designed to connect with the celestial movements that govern the universe. For centuries, it has been said that Holt's creation aligns with the stars during special astral events, awakening the powers hidden within its stones. Tonight, as the solstice aligns with a rare celestial convergence, the sculpture stirs from its usual stillness, ready to choose new guardians who will unlock its ancient mysteries.

Act One: The Circle of Beginnings: You and your teammates are modern-day archaeologists specializing in astrological artifacts, drawn to this site by the convergence of celestial events predicted in ancient manuscripts. As the night falls, you gather inside the inner circle of the Stone Enclosure, surrounded by the dark silhouettes of massive rocks that have stood the test of time. Suddenly, the ground beneath you hums with energy, and the air tingles with electricity as the stones begin to glow faintly under the moonlight. An ethereal voice resonates through the air.

"Welcome, seekers of the celestial truth. The Rings of Time have chosen this night to reveal their secrets to those who can decipher the language of the cosmos. You stand within the inner sanctum of the creation of the true guardian, Nancy Holt, encircled by stones that have witnessed the silent march of millennia. These stones are the keys to understanding the deeper connections between earth and the vast universe."

As the voice fades away, leaving a charged silence, your team is spurred into action. Your adventure through the Rings of Time has just begun... To prove yourselves as the true guardians of the Stone Rings, you must undertake a quest to find the Celestial Stones hidden within these circles. By finding these stones before the night ends, you will unlock the deeper magic of the Rings and secure your place as keepers of cosmic knowledge. Begin at the heart of the circle, and let the journey lead you outward to the realms where earth meets sky. Remember, each stone found brings you closer to the truth that binds all.
