Rings of Time: The Stone Quest!



How to Play:

Welcome to Rings of Time: The Stone Quest! This game is a thrilling team-based challenge that connects you to the history and structure of the renowned Stone Enclosure: Rock Rings. As you play, you'll embark on a journey through time and space, guided by the stars and the stones.


Objective:

Work together with your teammates to locate unique stones hidden within the concentric circles of the sculpture. Discover the stones within the given time to advance through the rounds and ultimately unveil the celestial mysteries of the sculpture.


Setup:

1. Choose a Moderator: Select one person to act as the moderator. They will confirm whether the stones you find are correct.

2. Starting Position: All players should gather inside the inner circle to begin the game.

3. Access the Game Panels:

  • Moderator: Click on the “Go to Moderator Panel” button to view the correct locations of the stones.
  • Players: Click on the “Go to Player Panel” button to receive your instructions and begin the search.
