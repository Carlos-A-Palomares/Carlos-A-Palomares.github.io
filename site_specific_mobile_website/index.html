Welcome!


This website hosts an interactive scavenger hunt and escape room type game that is centered around the intriguing Stone Enclosure: Rock Rings sculpture created by Nancy Holt.

Before you enter, please consider the following:

Explore and Enjoy: Beyond learning about the Stone Enclosure: Rock Rings, the game is intended to immerse you in a realm of fantasy and imagination, encouraging fun social interaction, and presence in the moment.

Relax and Listen: Should you opt to listen to the game's audio, take the moment to relax and engage fully with the narrative.

Recommended: Listen to the story first behind the creation of the Stone Enclosure: Rock Rings is suggested to better understand the game’s storyline. Go to the navigation bar and press the "About" link. Feel free to explore around the sculpture as you listen, or dive right into the game if you prefer.


Rings of Time: The Stone Quest!



How to Play:

Welcome to Rings of Time: The Stone Quest! Dive into an interactive team-based adventure within the mystical Stone Enclosure: Rock Rings, influenced by celestial events and history.


Objective:

As a team, locate five unique stones within the inner circle of the Stone Enclosure to unlock their magical powers and become the guardians of the Stone Rings.


Game Setup and Play:

1. Choose a Moderator: Before starting, select one team member to act as the moderator. This person will access the Moderator Panel, which displays the correct locations of each stone, allowing them to confirm or deny the players' findings.

2. Gather at the Starting Point: All players must assemble inside the inner circle of the Stone Enclosure to begin the quest.

3. Access Game Panels and Engage with the Introductory Story:

  • Moderator: Click the “Start” button to access the introductory story. After viewing or skipping the story by pressing "Proceed," you will see the map showing the locations of the stones, allowing you to confirm or deny the players' findings.

  • Players: Click the “Start” button to engage with the introductory story. After viewing or skipping the story by pressing "Proceed," you will you will be given details on the first stone to search for.

4. Begin the Search: Players start searching for the first stone. Once a stone is believed to be found, inform the moderator for verification.

5. Verification by Moderator: The moderator confirms whether the identified stone is correct. Upon confirmation, the moderator informs the players, who then press "Next" to proceed to the next stone.

6. Complete the Quest: Continue the search and verification process until all stones are found. Successfully locating all stones concludes the game, and players are celebrated as the new guardians of the Stone Rings.
