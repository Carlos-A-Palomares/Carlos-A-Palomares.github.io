Manneken Pis, Brussels, Belgium

Manneken Pis, often called the Peeing Boy, is a small bronze fountain sculpture in Brussels, Belgium, depicting a naked little boy urinating into the fountain's basin. Situated near the Grand Place in Brussels, the statue is a famous landmark and symbol of the city's irreverent sense of humor. Today, Manneken Pis not only draws tourists from around the world but also serves as a focal point for various local celebrations and events.
